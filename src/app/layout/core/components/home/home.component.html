The Importance of your Rental History and Credit Score.

Your payment history makes up about 35% of your credit score. Paying your bills on time that you are a responsible person. Regardless of whether or not you have been late on other payments in the past, reporting your rental payment history can make a big difference in improving your credit. With a good credit score, you will be rewarded with lower interest rates, better odds of approval, and lower deposits.
